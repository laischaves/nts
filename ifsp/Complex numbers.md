### Quick definitions
$$i=\sqrt{-1}$$

### Quick examples
Find the square root of -4:
$$\sqrt{-4} = \sqrt{4 \times (-1)} = \sqrt{4}\cdot \sqrt{-1} = 2\sqrt{-1} = 2i$$

Solve the equation:
$$x^2 + x + 1 = 0$$
$$\Delta = -3; x = \frac{-1 \pm \sqrt{-3}}{2}$$
$$\sqrt{-3} = \sqrt{3}\cdot\sqrt{-1} = i\sqrt{3}$$
